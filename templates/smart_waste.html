Waste refers to any material that is discarded because it no longer serves a useful purpose. It comes in various forms, including solid, liquid, or gas, and can be generated from households, industries, agriculture, and even natural processes. Proper waste management is essential for maintaining a clean, healthy, and sustainable environment.

There are several types of waste, each requiring different methods of disposal and recycling:
