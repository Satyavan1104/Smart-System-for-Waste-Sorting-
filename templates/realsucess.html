categories and disposal methods:

  • Recyclable: Items that can be processed and reused, like plastics, glass, and metals.
  • Compostable: Organic waste such as food scraps and yard waste.
  • Non-recyclable: Items that cannot be recycled, like certain plastics and mixed materials.
  • Dry waste: Waste that doesn't get wet and is usually non-recyclable, like packaging and textiles.
  • Wet waste: Waste that is biodegradable and can be composted or processed, like food waste.
